Here are the few Cooking Contest to particaipate

CWC

Cooku with Comali, also known by the acronym CWC, is an Indian Tamil-language comedy-based cooking competition show aired on Star Vijay and streams on Disney+ Hotstar where the contestants are paired each week with comedians (comalis) who are amateur cooks.

Prize Amount: 10 Lakhs
Language: Tamil

Master Chef

A nationwide quest to find the best 'home cook' draws out some of the most brilliant chefs from across India. The judges sample recipes from various cuisines that the contestants prepare in their search for India's 'MasterChef'

Prize Amount: 25 Lakhs
Language: Hindi
